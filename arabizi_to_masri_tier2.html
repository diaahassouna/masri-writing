Standardised words

A short dictionary of fixed high-frequency forms (inshallah, ya3ni, mesh, 3ashan, elhamdolillah…) is matched whole-word first, per the Framework's "consistency over derivation" rule.

Rule-based, not a language model — ambiguous cases (ث/ذ mergers, gemination, vowel length) may need manual review against the Framework's standardised word list.
